World's best scrambled Eggs

This recipe is a very special breakfast: just look at hte ingredients to see why it has to be tasted to be believed

Ingredients

The following ingredients make one serving.
  • 2 Eggs
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 1/3 cup of cream
  • A pinch of salt
  • Freshly milled black pepper
  • 3 fresh chives (chopped)


  1. Whisk eggs, cream & salt in a bowl
  2. Melt butter in a nonstick pan over high heat (taking care not to burn the butter)
  3. Pour the egg mixture into a pan, and wait until it starts setting around the edges of the pan (around 20 seconds)
  4. Using a wooden spatula, bring the mixture in to the center as if it were an omlette and let it cool for another 20 seconds
  5. Fold the contents in again, and leave for 20 seconds and then repeat until the eggs are only just done
  6. Grind a light sprinkling of freshly milled pepper over the eggs and blend in some choped fresh chives
